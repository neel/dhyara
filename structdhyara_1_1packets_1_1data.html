Detailed Description

A virtual packet, that is never sent accros the network.

Instead a data packet is splitted into multiple consecutive chunks. However after receiving all chunks of a data packet and data packet is constructed and passed to the appropriate callback.

Note
Every data packet have a packet id, which is included in the delivery report once that data packet reaches its destination.
